What We Provide to Our Employees

Efficient Workforce Management Through Our Customized Portal

We provide a Digital portal for employees, offering a range of features to streamline their work experience:

Features

Time Management:

Employees can adjust and arrange dates in their timesheets and submit work hours directly through the portal.

Client Verification:

Clients can view and verify employee hours, and provide signatures, ensuring the management at JL Mining knows that hours are authenticated as per the portal’s records.

Payroll:

Based on the verified hours, we handle employee payments.

Access to Information:

Employees can access on-site documents, view machinery manuals, and obtain client details and location information, including emergency contacts and the nearest town.

Training:

Online training is available within the portal, allowing employees to earn certificates that ease their workplace integration.

Safety Reporting:

The portal supports the reporting of hazards, incidents, and near-misses.
